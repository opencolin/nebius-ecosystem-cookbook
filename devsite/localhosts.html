What we cover

Per-event credits

$50–500 of Token Factory or AI Cloud credits per attendee, depending on event size and format.

Content + slides

Workshop decks, code repos, recorded video walkthroughs you can adapt to your audience.

Co-marketing

We promote your event across the Nebius newsletter, dev social channels, and partner network.

Event formats we support

Workshop

60–120 min hands-on. We supply the curriculum (Token Factory inference, fine-tuning, agents, deploy patterns).

Hackathon

Half-day to weekend. Token Factory credits per team, prize sponsorship, optional advocate as a judge.

Demo night

5-min lightning demos. Bring your community, show what you've built, swap notes.
